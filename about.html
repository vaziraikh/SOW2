Introduction

Climate Activist

Vazira Ikhtiyorova is a young and passionate climate activist from Uzbekistan. Born and raised in the capital Tashkent, she won a fully-funded scholarship from Islamic Development Bank (ISDB) to pursue her bachelor's in Computer Engineering at Marmara University in Istanbul, Turkiye.

Through her leadership and past experiences, she has been to the United States of America, South Korea, Italy, Egypt, and other countries to expand her network and exchange ideas with fellow participants. In addition, she is the founder of the Girls Development Summit of Chilanzar District in Uzbekistan where they support girls in STEM and STEAM. She has completed many internships at prestigious companies and trying to combat climate change.

Her passion and great enthusiasm sparked when she was accepted for Tunza Eco-Generation as a Regional Ambassador to Uzbekistan in 2019.
